Garri and groundnut

Description

Garri is pan fried cassava flakes which is very popular in West Africa. Garri is usually eaten in 3 ways

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup Garri
  • 3 cups water
    • 3 cubes of sugar 2tbs powdered milk pinch of salt

Toppings

  • 1/4 roasted groundnut
  • 1/4 cup coconut

Steps

  1. Pour water into the cereal bowl containing the dry Garri
  2. Sieve out any particle that floats
  3. Pour more fresh water into the Garri
  4. Then add sugar, pinch of salt (milk if using)
  5. Add the groundnuts or any other topping you prefer. Then relax and enjoy ?
